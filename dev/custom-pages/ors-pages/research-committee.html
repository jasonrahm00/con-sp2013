The Research Committee (RC) provides oversight, review, and recommendations related to the development and progress of research conducted by the faculty and students of the College of Nursing. The Research Committee meets on the first Monday of each month. Elected members of the RC are a Faculty Chair, two General Faculty Representatives, and a Student Representative. Also attending meetings are the Associate Dean of Research and an administrative support person.

Intramural Awards

For each award, the Research Committee decides (a) when to send out an application solicitation email, (b) what deadline to set for receipt of applications, (c) who will review the applications, and (d) whom to recommend for the award. A letter is then prepared for signatures by both the Chair of the Research Committee and Associate Dean of Research and Scholarship. This letter is then forwarded to the Dean for approval. Once approved, a speed type is set up for the award.

Doris Kemp Smith Award

Given to a faculty/PhD student team. A presentation is scheduled for the recipient to present her/his research following the close of this award. ORS facilitates this presentation by mailing invitations, preparing programs, reserving rooms, and managing the reception.

Boeker Award for Faculty Excellence in Research

Given every two years (in years with odd numbers) to a distinguished faculty member. The Research Committee selects a review committee to review the applications.
